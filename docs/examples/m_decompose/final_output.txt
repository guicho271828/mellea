**Event Strategy Document**

**1. Executive Summary**
- **Objective**: Foster teamwork and collaboration among employees through a comprehensive team-building event.
- **Key Highlights**: Incorporates workshops, interactive activities, networking opportunities, and wellness sessions to enhance professional development and employee satisfaction.

**2. Event Overview**
- **Purpose**: Strengthen team dynamics and improve communication within the organization.
- **Goals**: Enhance leadership skills, encourage innovation, and promote a healthier work-life balance.
- **Target Audience**: All employees across all departments, including management and junior staff.

**3. Planning Timeline**

**6 Months Before Event**
- **Week 1-2**: Finalize event goals and objectives
- **Week 3**: Budget approval and initial planning
- **Week 4**: Vendor selection process

**5 Months Before Event**
- **Week 5**: Venue booking confirmation
- **Week 6-7**: Detailed budget breakdown completion
- **Week 8**: Marketing campaign kickoff

**4 Months Before Event**
- **Week 9**: Finalize event schedule and logistics
- **Week 10**: Team formation strategy development
- **Week 11**: Catering plan finalization
- **Week 12**: Technology needs assessment

**3 Months Before Event**
- **Week 13-14**: Activity planning and logistics
- **Week 15**: Transportation arrangements
- **Week 16**: Registration process setup

**2 Months Before Event**
- **Week 17-18**: Marketing campaign intensification
- **Week 19-20**: Final review of all plans and budgets

**1 Month Before Event**
- **Week 21-22**: Staff training on roles and responsibilities
- **Week 23**: Final logistics checks and rehearsals
- **Week 24**: Contingency planning for potential issues

**2 Weeks Before Event**
- **Week 23**: Team assignments finalized
- **Week 23**: Participant surveys distributed
- **Week 24**: Last-minute preparations and confirmations

**1 Week Before Event**
- **Week 24**: Final setup and testing of AV equipment
- **Week 24**: Staff briefing sessions
- **Week 24**: Registration and check-in processes ready

**Event Day**
- **Day Before**: Set up venue, finalize logistics, conduct safety checks
- **On Event Day**: Execute the event schedule as planned

**Post-Event Activities**
- **1 Week After**: Send out participant surveys
- **2 Weeks After**: Conduct debriefing sessions with team leads
- **Month Post-Event**: Evaluate outcomes and plan for next year’s improvements

**4. Budget Breakdown**

**Venue Rental**: $15,000 - $25,000
**Catering**: $75 - $125 per person
**Entertainment & Activities**: $10,000 - $20,000
**Transportation**: $3,000 - $8,000
**Technology**: $5,000 - $10,000
**Marketing & Communications**: $5,000
**Miscellaneous/Contingency**: $2,500

*Total Estimated Budget*: **$50,000 - $125,000**

**5. Marketing and Communication Strategy**
- **Save-the-Date Announcements**: 4 months in advance via email and company intranet.
- **Formal Invitations**: 2 months before the event to all employees and stakeholders.
- **Weekly Reminder Emails**: Sent out starting one month prior to ensure high attendance.
- **Internal Campaigns**: Utilize internal newsletters, bulletin boards, and digital platforms for promotional activities.
- **Dedicated Slack Channel**: For real-time updates and discussions leading up to the event.
- **Executive Videos**: Highlighting vision and importance of the team-building exercise.
- **Mobile Event App**: For seamless scheduling, networking, and communication.

**6. Venue Selection**
- **Criteria Evaluation**: Based on capacity (100-300 people), accessibility for all employees, parking facilities, indoor/outdoor space availability, AV capabilities (projectors, sound systems), WiFi bandwidth for over 300 devices, catering facilities, accommodations for guests, and cost-effectiveness.
- **Selection Process**: Review of 10 venues followed by a site visit to ensure it meets the above criteria.

**7. Event Schedule**
- **8:00 - 9:00 AM**: Registration & Breakfast
- **9:00 - 9:30 AM**: Opening Ceremony (CEO Speech)
- **9:30 - 10:30 AM**: Icebreaker Activities (e.g., Human Bingo, Speed Networking)
- **10:30 - 12:30 PM**: Morning Workshops (Leadership, Innovation, Communication, Wellness)
- **12:30 - 2:00 PM**: Lunch Break
- **2:00 - 5:00 PM**: Team Challenges (Scavenger Hunt, Escape Rooms, Outdoor Adventures, Cooking Competition, Sports Tournaments)
- **5:00 - 5:30 PM**: Awards Ceremony
- **5:30 - 8:00 PM**: Dinner with Entertainment

**9. Team Formation Strategy**
- **Objective**: Create diverse teams comprising of employees from different departments and levels of seniority.
- **Process**:
  - Pre-event Survey: Collect data on dietary restrictions, preferences, and any special needs.
  - Team Assignments: Randomly form teams with a balanced mix of skills and personalities. Ensure representation across all levels (junior, mid-level, senior) and departments.
  - Communication Plan: Share team assignments and introductions in the Slack channel to build camaraderie.

**10. Workshop Content**
- **Leadership**: Case studies on successful leaders, interactive exercises on leading teams effectively.
- **Innovation**: Brainstorming sessions for innovative solutions, group challenges requiring creative thinking.
- **Communication**: Role-playing scenarios to improve communication skills, feedback sessions to enhance clarity and effectiveness in conveying messages.
- **Wellness**: Mindfulness practices, yoga sessions, stress management workshops.

**11. Catering Plan**
- **Breakfast**: Coffee station and assorted pastries/breakfast items.
- **Lunch**: Buffet-style with a variety of options including salads, main entrees (e.g., pasta, grilled chicken), sides, and vegetarian/vegan choices.
- **Afternoon Snacks**: Assorted cheeses, fruits, nuts, and light snacks for the team challenges.
- **Dinner**: Three-course meal featuring local cuisine or themed dishes to engage participants.

**12. Activity Logistics**
- **Equipment Lists**: List of all necessary equipment (projectors, sound systems, tables, chairs).
- **Setup Instructions**: Detailed steps for setting up each activity area.
- **Safety Protocols**: Emergency procedures and first aid station locations.
- **Facilitator Guides**: Manuals or guides for facilitators leading workshops and activities.
- **Scoring Systems**: Methods for scoring team challenges (e.g., creativity, teamwork, efficiency).
- **Rotation Schedules**: Timelines for rotating teams between different activities.

**13. Transportation Plan**
- **Charter Buses**: For larger groups traveling from out-of-town locations.
- **Parking Management**: Designated parking areas with clear signage and shuttle service available for those without personal vehicles.
- **Shuttle Service**: Daily shuttles to accommodate overflow or late arrivals/departures.

**14. Registration Process**
- **Online Portal**: Simple registration form capturing name, department, dietary restrictions, and any special needs.
- **On-site Check-in**: Name tags printed with employee information for easy identification.
- **Welcome Packets**: Include event schedule, map of the venue, swag bags, and any documents needed by participants (e.g., waivers).

**15. Safety Plan**
- **Insurance Coverage**: Ensure adequate coverage for all activities planned during the event.
- **First Aid Stations**: Strategically placed throughout the venue with trained staff on duty.
- **Evacuation Procedures**: Clearly marked exits and safe areas, communicated to all attendees.
- **Food Safety**: Allergen labeling for food items, cross-contamination prevention measures in place.

**16. Vendor Management**
- **Selection Criteria**: Quality of service, cost-effectiveness, reliability, customer reviews, and alignment with corporate values.
- **Contracts**: Detailed contracts outlining services provided, payment schedules, cancellation policies, and terms of use.
- **Payment Schedules**: Deposit upon contract signing followed by payments due on specific dates leading up to the event.
- **Evaluation Forms**: Post-event surveys for vendors to assess their performance.

**17. Technology Plan**
- **Event App**: For real-time updates, messaging, and navigation within the venue.
- **AV Equipment**: Projectors, microphones, screens, cameras for capturing moments during workshops and team challenges.
- **WiFi**: High-speed internet access capable of handling 300+ devices simultaneously.
- **Photography Services**: Professional photographers on-site to document key moments.
- **Live Streaming**: Options for live streaming selected parts of the event for remote participants.

**18. Decoration Strategy**
- **Signage**: Branding and directional signs throughout the venue.
- **Team Colors**: Incorporate company colors into banners, table settings, and photo backdrops.
- **Photo Backdrops**: Creative setups for group photos and individual shots during breaks.
- **Themed Zones**: Dedicated areas for different activities with thematic decorations.

**19. Wellness Sessions**
- **Mindfulness Workshops**: Guided meditation sessions to promote relaxation and stress reduction.
- **Yoga Classes**: Short yoga routines throughout the day to encourage physical activity and mental clarity.
- **Healthy Snacks**: Availability of nutritious snacks at designated breaks.

**20. Team Challenges**
1. Scavenger Hunt: Teams race through the venue solving puzzles and completing tasks.
2. Escape Rooms: Collaborative problem-solving challenges in themed rooms.
3. Outdoor Adventures: Physical activities like hiking, obstacle courses, or team sports.
4. Cooking Competition: Teams create dishes using limited ingredients within a set time frame.
5. Sports Tournament: Friendly matches promoting teamwork and friendly competition.

**21. Awards Ceremony**
- **Categories**: Best Teamwork, Most Innovative Idea, Outstanding Leadership, Funniest Moment (judged by peers).
- **Presentation**: Recognition speeches, photo opportunities, and sharing of key highlights from the day’s activities.

**22. Post-Event Activities**

- **Debriefing Sessions**: Team leads discuss what went well, areas for improvement, and how lessons learned can be applied in future events.
- **Outcome Evaluation**: Assess the effectiveness of team-building objectives such as improved communication, collaboration, and employee satisfaction.

**23. Contingency Planning**
