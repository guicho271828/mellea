**Unicorn Birthday Party Plan**

### **1. Shopping List**
- **Decorations**:
  - Unicorn Balloon Arch: Quantity: 2, Estimated Cost: $30
  - Unicorn Streamers (pink, purple, gold): Quantity: 1 roll ($15), Estimated Cost: $15
  - Paper Fans with Unicorn Design: Quantity: 20, Estimated Cost: $10

- **Food**:
  - Mini Pizza Bites (nut-free cheese and gluten-free crust): Quantity: 20 pieces ($40)
  - Fruit Skewers (strawberries, blueberries, grapes): Quantity: 1 tray ($15), Estimated Cost: $15
  - Veggie Sticks & Dip: Quantity: 1 bag of assorted veggies, 2 dips ($10)

- **Drinks**:
  - Juice Boxes (various flavors, nut-free): Quantity: 20 boxes ($20)
  - Water Bottles (serving water throughout the day): Quantity: 15 bottles ($15), Estimated Cost: $15

- **Party Favors**:
  - Unicorn Horn Keychains: Quantity: 15 pieces ($30)
  - Unicorns Stickers Packets: Quantity: 15 packets ($10)

- **Cake**:
  - Unicorn-Themed Cake (serves at least 20): Quantity: 1 cake ($150), Estimated Cost: $150

- **Total Estimated Cost**: Approximately $400

### **2. Party Games**
1. **Unicorn Balloon Arch**: Kids will enjoy helping create this whimsical arch using balloons in various unicorn-themed designs, encouraging teamwork and creativity.
   
2. **Pin the Tail on the Unicorn**: A spin-off of "Pin the Tail on the Donkey," where kids try to stick a cut-out tail on a large poster of a unicorn without looking, promoting laughter and engagement.

3. **Unicorn Pinata**: Fill a unicorn-shaped pinata with candies and small toys for excitement and surprises.
   
4. **Craft Station**: Set up an area where children can create their own unicorn horns using glitter, markers, and stickers in pink, purple, and gold colors, allowing them to express creativity.

5. **Story Time with Unicorns**: Gather the kids for a magical storytelling session featuring tales about unicorns, encouraging imagination and interaction.
   
6. **Unicorn Yoga or Dance Party**: Organize a fun yoga or dance session set to unicorn-themed music, promoting physical activity and relaxation in a playful setting.
   
7. **Fruit Skewer Unicorn Feeding**: Arrange for kids to create their own unicorn faces using fruit skewers and edible decorations such as icing and candy eyes.

### **3. Party Timeline**
- **12:00 PM - 1:00 PM**: Start the preparation by unpacking decorations, setting up a centerpiece with a unicorn balloon arch.
  
- **1:00 PM - 2:30 PM**: Unfurl the unicorn streamers around the room and hang paper fans on walls or tables.

- **3:00 PM**: Begin preparing food, assembling mini pizza bites using nut-free cheese and gluten-free crusts, slicing fruit skewers for healthy snacks, and preparing veggie sticks with dip.
  
- **4:30 PM**: Set out drinks, ensuring juice boxes are accessible while also having water bottles available to keep guests hydrated.

- **5:00 PM - 6:00 PM**: Finalize decoration setup and arrange activities around them. Ensure all decorations are in place for guests.

- **6:00 PM - 6:30 PM**: Guests arrive; welcome everyone and guide them towards the first activity area where they can start participating in games or crafts immediately.

- **6:30 PM - 7:15 PM**: Play "Pin the Tail on the Unicorn." This game engages kids as they try to stick their tail on a unicorn poster while blindfolded, promoting laughter and participation.
  
- **7:15 PM - 7:45 PM**: Serve food, offering mini pizza bites, fruit skewers, and veggie sticks for everyone to enjoy during this time slot.

- **7:45 PM - 8:30 PM**: Engage guests with a Unicorn Pinata activity. Fill the pinata with candies and small toys, then let the kids take turns trying to break it open.

- **8:30 PM - 9:15 PM**: Set up a Craft Station where children can create their own unicorn horns using glitter markers and stickers in various colors like pink, purple, and gold.

- **9:15 PM - 10:00 PM**: Transition into a Story Time with Unicorns session. Gather the kids for a magical storytelling experience featuring tales about unicorns to spark imagination.

- **10:00 PM - 10:45 PM**: Organize a Unicorn Yoga or Dance Party using unicorn-themed music, providing both physical activity and relaxation in a playful environment.

- **10:45 PM - 11:30 PM**: Activity for Fruit Skewer Unicorn Feeding. Kids can create their own unicorn faces by arranging fruit skewers with edible decorations like icing and candy eyes.

- **11:30 PM - 12:00 AM**: Serve the main course, which is a Unicorn-Themed Cake designed to serve at least 20 people.

- **12:00 AM - 12:30 AM**: Clean up the party area. Collect all trash, clear away decorations that are not needed for next day's events, and store or recycle used materials properly.

### **5. Backup Plan**
In case of rain, all activities will be moved indoors:
- **Tables and Seating**: Set up multiple tables for food, drinks, and crafts.
- **Food Stations**: Arrange food items on a central table or buffet style; label dishes clearly to avoid confusion.
- **Drink Stations**: Place water bottles, juice boxes, and cups near serving tables to encourage easy access.
- **Activity Areas**: Designate areas for different activities like craft stations, games, and relaxation spaces; ensure each area is safe, well-lit, and accessible.

### **6. Safety Guidelines**
- **Noise Levels**: Encourage guests to speak softly during storytelling or quiet activities.
- **Child Supervision**: Ensure adequate supervision of children at all times, especially during games and crafts.
- **Emergency Plan**: Have a designated first aid kit readily available for minor injuries or incidents; ensure that exits are clear and easily accessible.
